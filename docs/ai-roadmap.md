# AI Roadmap

Goal:
Transform uploaded meeting recordings into actionable insights.

Pipeline

Upload Audio
↓
Speech-to-Text
↓
Transcript Generation
↓
Meeting Summarization
↓
Action Item Extraction
↓
Store Results in MongoDB
↓
Display Insights on Dashboard
